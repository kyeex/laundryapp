# Privacy Policy Draft

Effective date: TBD

This Privacy Policy describes how [Business Name] collects, uses, and protects
information when customers, owners, and drivers use the LaundryApp mobile
application.

This draft should be reviewed by a qualified legal professional before public
launch.

## Information We Collect

We may collect:

- Name
- Email address
- Phone number
- Pickup and delivery address
- Laundry order details
- Service notes and delivery instructions
- Payment status and Stripe payment reference
- Driver assignment and delivery status
- Push notification token

We do not store full card numbers in the app. Payments are processed by Stripe.

## How We Use Information

We use information to:

- Create and manage user accounts
- Schedule laundry pickup and delivery
- Process and track laundry orders
- Assign orders to drivers
- Send order updates and notifications
- Request and track payment
- Provide customer support
- Improve operations and service quality

## Payment Information

Payment processing is handled by Stripe. The app may store payment status,
amount, and Stripe payment reference ids, but does not store full credit card
details.

## Notifications

Users may opt in to push notifications. Notifications may include order updates,
payment requests, pickup/delivery updates, and driver assignments.

Users can disable push notifications in their device settings.

## Information Sharing

We may share limited information with:

- Drivers, for assigned pickup and delivery work
- Stripe, for payment processing
- Firebase/Google Cloud, for authentication, database, functions, and hosting
- Expo, for push notification delivery

We do not sell personal information.

## Data Retention

We retain account and order information as needed to operate the laundry service,
resolve disputes, comply with legal obligations, and maintain business records.

## Security

We use Firebase Authentication, Firestore security rules, and role-based access
controls to protect app data. No system can be guaranteed completely secure.

## Children

The app is not intended for children under 13.
